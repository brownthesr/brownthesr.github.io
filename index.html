About Me

I am a student at Brigham Young University, majoring in Applied and Computational Mathematics with a passion for Machine Learning, particularly Deep Learning. My primary research interests lie in Geometric Deep Learning, focusing on Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Transformers.


I have hands-on experience with Large Language Models (LLMs) from my role as a research assistant, and I have worked on image processing during my internship with the Air Force. Recently, I contributed to cloud computing as a Software Developer Intern at AWS.


I am deeply enthusiastic about research and am particularly interested in the interpretability of machine learning models!
